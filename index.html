Read The Room was built on the concept of merging dynamic quality entertainment and media production together. Unlike typical DJs and entertainers, RTR is a premier three-component service offering photography, videography, and DJ for special events, formals, parties, or any venue we can provide our services to. Blending these services makes for an unforgettable experience that you can relive over and over again.  

Read The Room isn’t just any cliche name, we brand ourselves in our ability to “read the room” in every situation to capture every moment of the experience all while making our presence pleasurable and inviting for everyone.  

As we’ve grown through our experiences as entertainers we strive to create moments all while capturing them in the most unique, quality, and aesthetically pleasing way possible.
